Hello, I am Quintranela

As a little girl, I would love to watch my aunts in the kitchen baking a varity of pastries, my cousins and I would anticipate the moment when will it be time to eat. The aroma of each individual pastries would light up the house. Every ten minutes my cousins and I would pass by the oven waiting in anticipation. When they were finish, we couldn't wait to bite into one. My favourite was the chheese roll, what a delight it was saving every taste.

As the founder of 2S Pastry Delight, I have a vision, that every customer would experience what I felt as a child when I made that first bite. Every day, reshly made batches are made. Which was taught to me by my aunts because the taste is different.

Our service level extends beyond just providing fine pastry goods to the public. I believe the more we know about each other, the better we can work together to realize our common objectives.
